Nadi Astrology, also known as Nadi Jyotish, is a unique form of astrology that originated in ancient India. It is believed to have been written by the great sages and seers thousands of years ago. The word "Nadi" translates to "palm leaf" in Tamil, as these ancient texts were written on palm leaves.

What sets Nadi Astrology apart is its ability to provide incredibly accurate predictions about an individual's past, present, and future. The Nadi leaves are classified into different categories based on the thumb impressions of individuals seeking guidance. Each category corresponds to a specific Nadi grantha (book) that contains detailed insights and predictions.
